### Paladin — Retribution

- **Seal Twisting Fixed** — The swing timer was misread (`GetSwing` returns time _remaining_, not swing duration), which opened the twist window near the swing's midpoint — about 1.8s too early. Seal of Blood / Seal of the Martyr was cast far too soon and overwritten before the swing landed, so it procced on only a fraction of hits (~18 vs ~113 in a clean log) while wasting mana on re-seals. The twist now fires in the correct final-0.4s window, sustaining the seal across swings.
- **Heart of the Crusader Opener** — New `ret_opener_crusader` toggle (on by default). On the pull, casts Seal of the Crusader and Judges it to apply the +3% raid crit debuff; Crusader Strike refreshes it for the rest of the fight. Skips automatically if another paladin already has the debuff up, and won't get stuck if Judgement is on cooldown.
- **Avenging Wrath Modes** — New `ret_avenging_wrath` dropdown: `Never`, `On Cooldown`, `On Bosses`, or `During Burst` (default). No longer fires automatically on cooldown — it now waits for a burst trigger by default. Protection's Avenging Wrath is unchanged.
