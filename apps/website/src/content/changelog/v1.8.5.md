### Druid

- **Behind Detection Fix** — Reduced `IsBehind` error window from 1.5s to 0.3s (matching Rogue). The rotation now recovers almost immediately after repositioning behind the target instead of staying stuck on Mangle for 1.5s.
- **Mangle as Builder Setting** — New “Mangle as Builder” toggle in Cat > Advanced. When disabled, the rotation pauses if you’re not behind the target instead of falling back to Mangle — reposition and resume. On by default.
- **Powershift Min Mana** — Slider minimum lowered from 10% to 0%, allowing powershifts until completely OOM.
