### Warrior

- **Arms & Fury DPS Improvements** — Revised rotation priorities for higher sustained damage output.
- **Kebab Spec** — New warrior playstyle option.

### Paladin

- **Holy Healing Overhaul** — Reworked healing rotation with intelligent spell selection and downranking.

### Priest

- **Shadow Rotation Improvements** — Revised shadow priority logic for better DPS output.

### Druid

- **Resto Downranking** — Healing spells now correctly downrank based on HP deficit and mana efficiency.
- **Smarter Swipe AoE Threshold** — The +1 elite bump now only applies when elites are a minority in the pack. Pure elite packs cleave at your configured threshold.
- **Tick Tracker Powershift Fix** — Energy tick tracker now resets correctly on powershift instead of extrapolating from stale pre-shift timing.
- **Rip Min TTD Floor** — Slider minimum lowered from 8 to 0, allowing the TTD gate to be fully disabled.
