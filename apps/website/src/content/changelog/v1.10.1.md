### Warrior

- **Explicit Click Targeting** — Bloodthirst, Execute, and Victory Rush now ship with explicit `Click.autounit = "harm"` so they don't fall back to the framework's "both" auto-detect on TBC clients with incomplete `IsHarmfulSpell` metadata. Eliminates spurious "Target is not valid" errors during target transitions.
