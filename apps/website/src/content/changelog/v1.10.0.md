### Druid — Cat

- **Target Focus Behind Check** — New General toggle that treats "behind" as "target isn't targeting me" (`targettarget ~= player`) instead of the geometric position check. Useful in groups where the tank holds threat.
- **Powershift & Tick Optimization** — Powershift logic and energy-tick tracking aligned to wowsims TBC feral sim. New `Shift Delay Window` slider (0.5–1.5s, step 0.05).
- **Mangle Trick Wired Up** — The `use_mangle_trick` setting is now actually read by the code (previously dead). Includes the sim's skip clause: skipped when Rake Trick is on and Mangle isn't fully discounted by 2pT6. Also gated on Mangle being off cooldown so it doesn't block Shred while waiting on Mangle CD.
- **Bite-Aware Shift Delay** — The "is a tick worth waiting for?" calculation now counts regular Ferocious Bite as a valid next action at max CP (energy 15–19 + 5 CP correctly delays the shift since the tick lands at 35–39, Bite-eligible).
- **WolfsheadShred Restored** — Position-agnostic Wolfshead powershift back in the priority list; respects the `!rip_next` gate to avoid burning a shift when a tick will reach Rip cost.
- **/fticks Default Off** — Energy-tick debug printing no longer spams chat on load. Toggle on with `/fticks`.

### Druid — Balance

- **Arcane-Immune Handling** — Skip Starfire/Moonfire vs arcane-immune targets, route to Wrath/Insect Swarm. Shared `NS.ARCANE_IMMUNE` table in core.
- **Starfire Downranking** — Auto-downrank Starfire to rank 6 in mana-conservation tier (mirrors wowsims).
- **Insect Swarm Target Filter** — Always-on with target classification dropdown (all/elites+/bosses/off). 4p T5 (Nordrassil Regalia) forces IS at lowest mana tier.
- **Caster Stance Guard** — Emergency/proactive heals and dispels now gated on caster stance so they don't fire in Bear/Cat/Moonkin.

### UI / Settings

- **Fractional Slider Step Fix** — Settings sliders with non-integer `step` now snap to multiples of step instead of rounding to nearest integer.
