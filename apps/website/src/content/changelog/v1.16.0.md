### Paladin — Retribution

- **Configurable Twist Lead** — New _Twist Lead_ slider (Retribution > Seal Twisting, default 0.40s, range 0.25–0.80s) controls how early before your swing the rotation twists into Seal of Blood. Raise it to compensate for latency — the whole Command-prep → twist sequence shifts earlier together.
- **Crusader Strike No Longer Clips the Twist** — Once Seal of Command was prepped, Crusader Strike and fillers (Hammer of Wrath, Exorcism, Consecration, Holy Wrath) could still fire in the ~1s before the swing, running their GCD into the twist window so the swing procced Command instead of twisting into Blood. They now hold once a twist is committed and only fire while Blood is the resident seal, sharply increasing twist count.
