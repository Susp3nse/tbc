### Paladin — Retribution

- **Seal Twist Inverted — Martyr Stays Up** — The rotation kept Seal of Command as the resident seal and only flashed Seal of Blood / Seal of the Martyr in for the twist, so it kept falling back to Command after every Judgement. That inverted the seal uptimes versus a strong ret (Command-heavy instead of Martyr-heavy) and cost a lot of damage. Now Blood/Martyr is the resident seal — the guaranteed-damage seal you also Judge — and Seal of Command is briefly twisted in before each swing, then dropped back to Martyr.
- **Twist Seal Rank** — New `ret_twist_seal_rank` dropdown (default Rank 1). The twisted-in Seal of Command was casting at max rank (280 mana) instead of Rank 1 (65 mana) because the rank wasn't forced — a steady mana drain. Rank 1 is recommended: the proc damage is identical at every rank.
- **Crusader Strike On Cooldown** — Crusader Strike was firing every ~9-11s instead of on its 6s cooldown, because it was blocked for the entire last ~1.5s of every swing. It now only yields during the 0.4s twist window and fires near its cooldown.
