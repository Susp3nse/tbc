### Hunter — Ranged

- **Adaptive DPS Engine** — The ranged rotation is now a single per-tick engine that weighs every shot by its damage-per-cast and rebalances automatically as your haste changes, replacing the old fixed weave modes. Stings keep their pre-priority, and when no special beats waiting it simply lets Auto Shot ride.
- **Wing Clip Breaks Kael'thas Mind Control** — With Infinity Blade equipped, targeting a Mind-Controlled ally now suggests only Wing Clip to break the control with melee damage, suppressing the rest of the rotation until they're free.
- **Sharper Immunity & Enrage Handling** — PvP immunities now follow the framework's maintained categories, PvE immunity is matched by spell ID (fixing a name collision that wrongly held DPS on Kael'thas), and Tranquilizing Shot reliably strips creature enrages. Feign Death also skips the Netherstrand Longbow.
- **Manual Raptor Queue** — New `/menagerie raptor` command forces a single Raptor Strike weave window on demand for melee-weaving hunters.
- **Healthstone Actually Fires** — The recovery middleware was registering the Master Healthstone as a spell instead of a bag item, so it never used it; corrected to match every other class.
