### Druid

- **Cat Rotation Sim Alignment** — Major refactor to align cat rotation priorities and energy logic with the wowsims TBC feral simulator.
- **Priority Reorder** — Bite Trick and Rake Trick now check before Mangle debuff maintenance, matching sim behavior.
- **Smart Shift Delay** — New context-aware setting that delays powershifts up to 1 second for an energy tick, but only when the tick will actually reach a useful threshold.
- **Energy Pooling Toggle** — New setting to enable/disable energy pooling for Rip. Mangle debuff no longer pools energy.
- **Swing Timer Delay Toggle** — New setting to enable/disable swing timer delay on ability casts.
- **Bite Trick Improvements** — Removed TTD gate (was preventing bite trick on dying targets). Now uses sim-matched 0.1s tick threshold.
- **Rake Trick Improvements** — Now uses sim-matched 1.0s tick threshold.
- **Sim-Optimal Defaults** — Default settings updated to match wowsims optimal feral config: 5 CP rip, elites only, no rake maintenance, no bite execute, no rake trick, no swing delay.
- **Cleanup** — Removed Cat_ExecuteBite (consolidated into FerociousBite), removed dead "avoid idle" fallback, removed redundant fb_max_energy check from Shred.
