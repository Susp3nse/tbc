### Warrior — Protection

- **Thunder Clap Stays in Defensive Stance** — The Prot rotation was dancing to Battle Stance every time it refreshed Thunder Clap, then swapping back — an unnecessary stance flicker mid-tanking. In TBC, Thunder Clap is castable in Defensive Stance, so it now fires in place with no stance swap.
