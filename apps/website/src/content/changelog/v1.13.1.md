### Paladin — Retribution

- **Correct Twist Direction** — TBC seal twisting is one-way: you can only twist _from_ Seal of Command _into_ Seal of Blood / Seal of the Martyr. v1.13.0 had it backwards (flashing Command in while Blood was resident, which doesn't twist). Now Blood/Martyr is the resident seal that rides through Crusader Strike, fillers and Judgement, and Seal of Command is briefly prepped before each swing so you twist into Blood — matching how a strong ret parses (Martyr-dominant uptime).
- **Smarter Judgement Timing** — Judgement of Blood is now cast right before prepping Command for the twist, instead of mid-cycle. Since Judgement consumes the seal, the old timing forced an extra Seal of Blood re-cast that could clip the next twist; judging at the prep point uses up the seal you were about to drop anyway.
